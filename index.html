Where did GAVST Graduates go?

Choose a year from the layers on the top left of the map to see where the graduates of the Geraard Adriaan van Swieten Horticultural School were working from 1900 to 2000. Click on the garden hares to learn more.

Hello! Map by Nina Litsios (July 2025). Base layer of map is "cartodb positron". The hare icons used are from icons8.com. The data was retrieved from the address lists of the VOLDGAVST yearbooks published in 1900, 1925, 1950, 1975, and 2000.
